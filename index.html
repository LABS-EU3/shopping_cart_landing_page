How it works

Answer six questions to get registered.

Add your products and share your store URL with your customers.

Only people you share your store with can see it, which protects you from competitors copying your catalog.

You can receive payment in new and innovative ways, including via Stripe, USSD or in person!

Basic functionality will always be free to use for both buyers and sellers.

Pro functionality will be added as the app evolves.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

PureRetail is free for both buyers and sellers.

How do I know I can trust buys / sellers on this app?

We put you in direct contact via WhatsApp. It's up to you to decide whether you trust them. We do not recommend sending goods or payments to buyers / sellers you've not met in person.

What kind of goods can I buy / sell?

Anything legal. We reserve the right to remove listings that receive complaints or are illegal.
